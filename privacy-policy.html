Privacy Policy

Horizure ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you visit our website or interact with our services. By using our site, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect both personal and non-personal information, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name; project inquiries, quotes, and service requests; billing and payment details (when applicable).
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, usage data (pages visited, time spent), device type and identifiers.
  • Cookies & Tracking: Cookies to enhance user experience; tools like Google Analytics and Meta Pixel to monitor website traffic and marketing performance.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and deliver our web development and marketing services.
  • To respond to inquiries, requests, and support messages.
  • To improve our website performance and user experience.
  • To send relevant updates, newsletters, or promotional content (only with your consent).
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Analyze website performance and user behavior.
  • Customize content and ads.
  • Improve user navigation and experience.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our business (such as web hosting, payment gateways, email marketing tools), under strict confidentiality agreements.

We may also disclose information when required by law, to protect our rights, or to comply with a legal obligation.

Data Security

Horizure uses industry-standard security practices to protect your data, including secure servers and firewalls, SSL encryption, and limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only. While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage users to exercise caution when submitting sensitive information online.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your information.
  • Withdraw consent to receive marketing communications.
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.
